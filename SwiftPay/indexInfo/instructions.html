Instructions

1
Step 1: Log in to SwiftPay Payroll. Admin account for generating payroll & employee account for attendance for emplyoee.
2
Step 2: In admin account, add employee base on their information Name, Hire Date,Position ID,Department ID,Deduction ID,Job Status ID. If you can't find the Position,Department base on the information given, then just simply add it in their respective tab.
3
Step 3: In order to generate payroll we need hours work from the employee base on their attendance. Employee can attendance by simply logging in their account. In there they can see the attendance tab.
4
To generate payroll go to Payroll Tab. Click the ADD button and fill up the employee ID and select if you generate payroll for first half or second half of the month.
5
When you already add payroll click the Equals button to calculate and generate payroll.
